IRON MAN

Iron Man is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby.

ARMOR

The Iron Man Armor Mark LXXXV was the most advanced and powerful armor Tony Stark ever created.

ARC REACTOR

The Arc Reactor was a palladium-core power source used in the original suit, later upgraded with new technology.
